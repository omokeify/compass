# COMPASS // STRATEGIC ROADMAP & MONETIZATION BLUEPRINT

This document outlines the next operational phase for the **Compass Platform**, focusing on the transition from a "Community Resource" to a "High-Intensity Premium Economy."

---

## 1. Monetization: The "Unlock Premium" Signal Gateway
**Objective**: Transform the Compass into a revenue-generating intelligence engine by gating high-fidelity signals.

### 📡 Premium Signal Gating
*   **Alpha Corner HUD**: Implement a "Blurred Preview" mode for non-premium members. High-alpha signals (e.g., L2 launches, token audits) require a **"Commander Pass"** to reveal.
*   **Skill Marketplace Priority**: Gigs with budgets >$5,000 are exclusively visible to Premium Members for the first 24 hours.
*   **Revenue Models**:
    *   **The Genesis Pass**: A one-time NFT or token-gated lifetime access.
    *   **The Hub Retainer**: A recurring $USDC or $USD subscription (Stripe/Base).

---

## 2. Industrial Reputation Economy
**Objective**: Create a "Vet-First" ecosystem where the highest quality talent receives the highest visibility.

### 🏆 Reputation Governance
*   **Reputation Scores**: Algorithmically derived from mission completion, successful gig delivery, and peer-review high-fives.
*   **The Talent Tier HUD**: Display a "Vetted Status" badge on profiles that have successfully delivered 3+ projects in the Hub.
*   **Skill Verification**: Integrated assessments or "Proof of Work" audits to unlock higher-tier skill badges.

---

## 3. Real-Time Protocol Ignition
**Objective**: Eliminate manual refreshes by moving the entire platform to a live, broadcasting data-flow.

### ⚡ Supabase Realtime Implementation
*   **Live Intel Bell**: Subscribe every member's Dashboard to the `alpha_bounties_gigs` table using `.on('postgres_changes', ...)`. This ensures as you click "Broadcast" in the Admin HUB, every member's bell rings instantly.
*   **Global Pulse**: A live "Activity Ticker" showing real-time registrations and marketplace inquiries as they happen.

---

## 4. Advanced Admin Governance Center
**Objective**: Expand the Admin Dashboard into a total Command & Control center for the ecosystem.

### 🛠️ Governance Controls
*   **The Registry Auditor**: A bulk-management view for approving talent listings and verifying member technical stacks.
*   **Signal Analytics**: A high-fidelity dashboard for tracking which Alpha signals and Gigs are receiving the most engagement.
*   **Automated Broadcasting**: Schedule alpha signals and ecosystem updates for targeted delivery.

---

## 5. Web3 Native Integration
**Objective**: Forge a deeper connection between the Hub and the on-chain economy.

### 🔗 Chain-Scale Identity
*   **Lens/Farcaster Connect**: Integrate social profiles into the Member Registry for verified identity signals.
*   **On-Chain Payments**: Direct settlement of marketplace contracts via **Circle SDK** or **Base Mainnet**.
